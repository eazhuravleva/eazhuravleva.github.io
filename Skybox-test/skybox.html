About SKYBOX

The main task of the game is to test your resilience to failures that will follow you to the last level.

- Are you ready to fly by, fly or not at all reach the finish line dozens of times in a row to the driving music?
- Are you ready to lose your cube over and over again due to a sharp turn of the skate?
- Do you have the patience to constantly lose control on uneven surfaces?
If yes, then in the game you will find:
1.24 Levels.
2. A variety of 6 skate skins
3.6 dissimilar cube skins
4. Energetic music that will give you the strength to overcome failures!
